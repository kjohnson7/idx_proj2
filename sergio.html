Hours of Class

This visualization shows the data of the classes I had between January 25th and February 7th of 2017. I decided to use different sized pencils to show the amount of hours I’ve spent in class.

Hours of Sleep

This visualization shows the data of my hours of sleep between January 25th and February 7th of 2017. I decided to create a table that included all 24 hours of a day and paint each cell based on my activity. Purple is for “sleep”, and blue is for “awake.”
