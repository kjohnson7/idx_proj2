Interesting Moments



I wanted to visualize the data I collected for the steps I had taken and present it playing with the length of the rounded shapes I chose for each day of the week. Since this visualization was grouping data from 2 weeks, I wanted to incorporate ways to consider a progressive disclosure of more specific data. This way, you could see the total by looking at the graph and discover more when interacting with it.

Defining Interactions

It is important to me to keep things simple. Sometimes when thinking of animations or user interaction feedback, things can get too busy or even confusing. I decided to make the colored blocks I used for each day interactive to reveal detailed information when hovering over it. After showing this to other students at CCA, I was suggested to also make the rounded shapes in the graph behave the same way. This means that the user can either hover over the colored blocks or the rounded shapes to see more information about that day.
